Step 1

Setup the Bookmarklet

Make sure your Bookmarks Bar is visible in Chrome (press Ctrl + Shift + B or Cmd + Shift + B to toggle it). Then, drag the button below directly into your bookmarks bar:

🚀 Drag Me to Bookmarks Bar
Step 2

How to Use

  1. Open Chrome and navigate to any job application page (like Greenhouse, Lever, etc.).
  2. Click the 🚀 Drag Me to Bookmarks Bar bookmark you just added.
  3. The script will instantly autofill your name, email, phone, LinkedIn, GitHub, college, and other fields, and highlight them in green.
  4. Drag & drop your compiled CV PDF into the file field and hit Submit!
